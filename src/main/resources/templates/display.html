cybervora.com – The Simple Internet Clipboard

About cybervora.com

cybervora.com is your go-to tool for quick and easy data sharing between internet-connected devices.

How It Works

  1. Choose any URL beginning with cybervora.com (e.g., cybervora.com/youruniqueid).
  2. Add your content—text, links, or any data.
  3. Access the same URL on another device to retrieve the information.

Features

  • One-Time Use: By default, cybervora are deleted after being accessed.
  • Extendable Storage: Keep your cybervora for up to 30 days if needed.

cybervora.com makes seamless data sharing between devices simple and fast.
